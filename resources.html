Inclusion of Disability Data

Understanding the global and local impact of disabilities is crucial for creating an inclusive society.

Global/Local Stats

Approximately 15% of the world’s population, or an estimated 1 billion people, live with disabilities. The most common types of disabilities include visual, hearing, mobility, and cognitive impairments.

Source: WHO, CDC
Employment Rates among PWD

The employment rate of people with disabilities (PWD) is significantly lower than those without disabilities. Only 36% of working-age PWD are employed compared to 75% of people without disabilities.

Source: Bureau of Labor Statistics
Impact of Technology

Technological advancements such as assistive devices, AI-driven tools, and accessibility features in mainstream tech have improved quality of life, accessibility, and independence for many PWD.

Source: UN
